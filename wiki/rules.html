Rules of Basketball

Standard basketball court layout and dimensions

Basketball has simple rules that make it easy to understand and play.

Basic Rules

  • Two teams of 5 players each
  • Score by shooting ball through opponent's hoop
  • Must dribble while moving with the ball
  • Games have 4 quarters or 2 halves
  • Field goals = 2 points (3 if from beyond the arc)
  • Free throws = 1 point each
